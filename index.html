About Payton

Payton R Macdonald is a creative thinker who is a full-stack jack-of-all-trades. He learns for a living and continues to make clean, polished, and optimized websites and applications.

He started as a freelance graphic designer that graduated with a bachelor’s degree at Brigham Young University, majoring in Communications: Advertising. After a social media internship and a job as a Marketing Assistant he found a passion for web development and became a Full-stack Developer at Boise CodeWorks Coding Bootcamp.

Now with digital marketing and SEO experience mixed with an aptitude for tech and code, Payton aims to invent the next best user experience and design products a perfectionist would be proud of.
